Movie Rating Analysis

IMDb · Metascore · Netflix  |  COM-480 Data Visualization · Group Sarah Le Duc

Project Goal

We explore how movie ratings reflect quality, popularity, and platform context — and where rating systems disagree. Using the IMDb Top 1000 and the Netflix catalog we build three complementary views that let you dig into audience-vs-critic gaps, shifting genre trends, and the relationship between popularity and quality.

The Questions

  • Where do audience scores (IMDb) and critic scores (Metascore) diverge most?
  • How has genre popularity shifted from the 1920s to today?
  • Do highly-rated films attract more votes, or is quality independent of popularity?
  • How does Netflix content compare to IMDb's curated top tier?
